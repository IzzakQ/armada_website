# Product Overview

This is a logistics company website for "АРМАДА" (Armada), a Russian logistics service provider.

## Purpose

Marketing website to showcase logistics services and capture leads for freight delivery across Russia and CIS countries.

## Key Features

- Service showcase (freight delivery, warehousing, customs clearance)
- Company information and values
- Lead generation form with route and cargo type selection
- Mobile-responsive design
- Russian language content

## Target Audience

Businesses requiring freight transportation and logistics services in Russia and CIS regions.
